About

About Us

At Belle & Carrie Rehabilitation Yoga, we focus on healing and restoring balance through yoga. Our sessions are designed for individuals recovering from injuries, dealing with chronic pain, or seeking to improve overall well-being.

Our Services

We offer personalized rehabilitation yoga classes tailored to all skill levels. Our approach combines gentle poses, breathing techniques, and relaxation methods to support your healing journey.

Join Our Community

Be a part of a supportive and nurturing community. Share your journey, connect with others, and experience the benefits of yoga in a welcoming environment.

Contact Us

Have questions or need more information? Reach out to us, and we'll be happy to help you take the first step toward a healthier, more balanced life.
